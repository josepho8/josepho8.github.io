About Me

Hi there! My name is Joseph Ogunleye. I combine technical expertise in data analysis with hands-on experience in finance and accounting to uncover meaningful business insights. With a Master’s in Data Science and Analytics and a Bachelor’s degree in Finance, I’ve built a strong foundation in both data and business strategy.

My diverse background has equipped me with a wide range of transferable skills, including financial and data analysis, project management, and advanced proficiency in tools like Python, SQL, Tableau, Excel, and R-Studio. I’m also known for being a collaborative team player with strong communication skills and a passion for continuous learning.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between technical solutions and strategic goals. During my internship at ATB Financial, I applied my skills to real-world challenges—building dashboards, analyzing pricing data, and delivering insights that shaped key business decisions.

I’m passionate about using data to solve problems, uncover opportunities, and support better decision-making. Thanks for stopping by—please feel free to connect so we can explore opportunities to grow and collaborate!
